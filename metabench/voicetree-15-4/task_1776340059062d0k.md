- Turn 1 = plan turn only. - `PLAN_STATE` is free-form TEXT (not schema'd). The user explicitly asked for todo/subtask plan state as a string.
- 30-min total budget, 600s per-subtask budget, MAX_EXEC_TURNS=4.
- Log-prob gap thresholds: ā‰¤0.01 nats, ā‰¤0.1 nats, ā‰¤0.5 nats. Gap = |log(p*) - log(p_hat)|.
